Privacy Policy

Short version: Loopfall does not collect, store, or share your personal data. There are no analytics, no ads, and no tracking. Your high scores are yours.

What we collect

Nothing that personally identifies you. Loopfall stores only gameplay data locally on your device: high scores, statistics (total runs, total taps, etc.), unlocked modes, and your chosen theme and language. This data never leaves your device unless you enable cloud sync (see below).

Third-party services

Loopfall uses the following platform services. Each is optional and governed by its provider's own privacy policy.

Apple platforms (iOS, iPadOS, tvOS, macOS)

  • iCloud Key-Value Store — syncs your high scores and statistics across your Apple devices when you're signed into iCloud. Data is stored in your personal iCloud account, not ours. We never see it.
  • Game Center — if you sign in, your scores can be submitted to public leaderboards and achievements unlocked. Sign-in is optional; the game runs fully without it.

Steam (PC)

  • Steam Cloud — syncs your saves across machines signed into your Steam account.
  • Steam leaderboards — if enabled, high scores may be submitted to public global leaderboards associated with your Steam username.

Children's privacy

Loopfall does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game is safe for all ages and contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no social communication features.

Analytics & tracking

None. Loopfall does not use any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, crash reporting, or advertising identifiers. We don't know how many people played today, how far they got, or what device they used — and we like it that way.

Website

This website (hosted on GitHub Pages) does not set any cookies and does not run analytics. Embedded YouTube videos use youtube-nocookie.com. Google Fonts may log a request when the page first loads; no user information is sent.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will reflect that. The change history lives in the public repository for this website.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@loopfall.game.
