Short description

Loopfall is a one-tap arcade runner set inside a neon torus. Dodge gates in Pure Hell, blast through obstacles in Blitz mode. Hypnotic visuals, handcrafted themes, and a skill ceiling that keeps you coming back. On iPhone, iPad, Apple TV, Mac, and Steam.

Long description

Loopfall is a hypnotic arcade runner that drops you inside a neon torus and dares you to keep rolling. Tap left, tap right — that's the whole game. But the speed climbs, the gates multiply, and the descent never ends.

Two modes push you in different directions. Pure Hell is the original: constant speed, constant gates, survive as long as you can. Blitz turns the torus into a shooting gallery — buttons to blast, gates to open, tiers to climb, and a streak to keep alive.

Built on custom shaders that mimic CMYK channel separation, Loopfall is unapologetically loud — eight handcrafted themes auto-cycle as you play, from cool indigo voids to acid-green fever dreams. It runs at 60fps on every supported device and remembers exactly nothing about you beyond your high scores.

Available on iPhone, iPad, Apple TV, Mac, and Steam, with iCloud and Steam Cloud sync, seven languages, and Game Center / Steam leaderboards out of the box.
